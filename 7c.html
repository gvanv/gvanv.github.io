What is the better path? PA school or Med school?

Students often ask which path would be more beneficial for them. It is a hard decision and one that can decide a lot in your life. Here are some tools to help you with making that decision easier!

Pros of Med School

- More Specialized

- Higher Salary

- More Recognized

Cons of Med School

- Longer Schooling

- Less Patient Contact

- High Student Loans

Pros of PA School

- Ability to Switch Specialities

- Shorter Schooling

- Enter Workforce Quicker

Cons of PA School

- Lower Salary

- Less Respected

- More Competitive
